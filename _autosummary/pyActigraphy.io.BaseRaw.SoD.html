pyActigraphy.io.BaseRaw.SoD¶

BaseRaw.SoD(freq='5min', whs=4, start='12:00:00', period='5h', algo='unanimous')¶

Sleep over Daytime

Apply a sleep scoring algorithm over a ‘period’ period starting from the activity onset time (AOT)
Parameters:
  • freq (str) – Alias of a timeseries offset. Default: ‘5min’
  • whs (int) – Window half size. Default: 4
  • start (str) – Start time of the period of interest. Default: ‘AonT’ Supported times: ‘AonT’, ‘AoffT’, any ‘HH:MM:SS’
  • period (str) – Timedelta. Default: ‘10h’
  • algo (str) –

    Sleep scoring algorithm to use. Default: ‘unanimous’. Supported algorithms:

    ’ck’, ‘sadeh’, ‘scripps’, ‘majority’, ‘unanimous’
